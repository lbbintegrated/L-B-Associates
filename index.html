Company Overview

LBB Integrated Company is a duly registered company with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) of the Federal Republic of Nigeria, with its head office located in Lagos.

The company serves as the holding company of LBB Properties, specializing in real estate development, property acquisition, and strategic investment solutions.

In all real estate dealings, the names LBB Integrated Company and LBB Properties may be used interchangeably without prejudice, representing the same trusted brand and operational integrity.
